About Amazon Clone

Our Story

Welcome to Amazon Clone, a demonstration project showcasing modern e-commerce capabilities. We've built this platform to demonstrate the implementation of essential online shopping features.

Our Mission

Our mission is to demonstrate the possibilities of e-commerce development using Python, Flask, and modern web technologies. We aim to provide a learning resource for developers interested in building their own e-commerce platforms.

Key Features
  • Secure user authentication and registration
  • Product catalog with search functionality
  • Shopping cart and checkout process
  • Order tracking and history
  • Product reviews and ratings
